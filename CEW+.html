Center for the Education of Women +

Welcome to the Center for the Education of Women+ (CEW+) at the University of Michigan! CEW+ is dedicated to empowering students by providing an array of resources, support services, and educational opportunities designed to enhance your academic journey. Whether you're seeking guidance on career development, scholarships, or navigating university life, CEW+ is here to support you every step of the way. Our commitment to diversity, equity, and inclusion ensures that all students, regardless of background or life circumstances, have access to the tools they need to succeed. Explore our workshops, counseling services, and community events to make the most of your time at U-M and achieve your educational goals.

Our Mission

Our mission is to enhance the educational and professional experiences of women and other marginalized groups. We strive to foster an inclusive environment that encourages learning, leadership, and community engagement.

Services and programs

* Scholarships and Fellowships:

We offer financial assistance to support students in achieving their academic goals.

* Career and Education Counseling:

Our team provides guidance and resources to help individuals navigate their career paths and educational opportunities.

* Workshops and Events:

CEW+ hosts a variety of workshops and events designed to build skills, knowledge, and networks.

* Advocacy and Outreach:

We advocate for policies and practices that promote equity and inclusion across the university and beyond.

Get Involved

Join our community by participating in our programs, attending events, or volunteering. Together, we can create a supportive network that uplifts and empowers.
